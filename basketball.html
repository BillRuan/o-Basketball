What Is Basketball?

Stereotypical Picture:

You may think of basketball as a fast paced, hardcore, intense pseudo-acrobatic sport. You think it's all about having a big frame and explosive legs. Let me tell you something. You don't develop those until you reach your optimal potential. There is way more than what meets the eye.
