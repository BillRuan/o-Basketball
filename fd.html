IMPROVING DRIBBLING AND BALL HANDLING

The primary fundamentals to moving the ball around and getting to your goal is dribbling. Dribbling the ball involves exerting a force from your fingers to push the ball down to make it bounce back up. There are many tircks and variations, as opponents will attempt to stop and block you. If you're lucky and explsoive/swift enough, you can fake your opponent out and make them fall over. Unfortunately, injuries are common, such as broken ankles. Certain precautions are recommended, such as dynamic stretching and ankle protectors.
